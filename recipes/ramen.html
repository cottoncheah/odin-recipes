Chilli Oil Ramen

Description

An easy, cheap and tasty student meal

Ingredients

  • Ramen noodles
  • Seasoning packet
  • Vegetable oil

Steps

  • Add all sauce ingredients to a bowl
  • Boil Noodles or pasta for 3 mins leave to the side
  • Heat up oil on medium high heat for 3-4 mins until hot but not smoking
  • Pour oil into the bowl and add noodles
  • Mix and serve with toasted sesame seeds

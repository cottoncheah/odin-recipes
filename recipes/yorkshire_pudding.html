Yorkshire Pudding

Description

A traditional northern english recipe

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Vegetable oil

Steps

  • Preheat oven 200 degrees for 10 mins
  • Mix equal parts flour and eggs together
  • Slowly whisk in milk
  • Season with salt and milk
  • Place in fridge
  • Pour generous amount of oil into an oven pan, enough to cover the bottom and place in oven for 10 mins
  • After 10 mins pour batter mixture into the oven pan - reaction between hot oil and cold batter essential
  • Cook for 25 mins without opening oven

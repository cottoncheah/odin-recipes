Blueberry and Banana Pancakes

Description

This is a healthy and tasty breakfast recipe

Ingredients

  • Bananas
  • Blueberries
  • Eggs
  • Flour
  • Baking Powder

Steps

  1. Mash bananas and crack 3 eggs into mashed bananas
  2. Gradually add flour until desired consistency
  3. Add baking powder and pinch of salt
  4. Pan fry, adding blueberries to pancakes while the first side is browning
